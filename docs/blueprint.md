# **App Name**: Ode Weaver

## Core Features:

- Poem Generation: Generate poems from user prompts, styled to evoke the spirit of 'Dead Poets Society'. Uses AI to emulate human-like expressiveness and refine the generated text.
- Aesthetic Formatting: Apply beautiful and thematically-fitting formatting to generated poems. It incorporates stylistic elements reminiscent of classic literature, such as ornate fonts, parchment-like backgrounds, and elegant layouts.
- Theme Selection: Allow users to choose from various aesthetic themes inspired by 'Dead Poets Society' such as: Romantic, Naturalistic, and Classic.
- Professional Presentation: Refine poems by improving grammar, vocabulary and stylistic choices with generative AI tools.
- Inspirational Quote Display: Display famous quotes about poetry and life from movies.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Muted gold (#CDB38B) to evoke a sense of timelessness and sophistication, reminiscent of old parchment and classic literature.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), a very light desaturation of the primary hue.
- Accent color: Deep brown (#8B4513), analogous to gold but contrasting in brightness, providing depth and a classical feel.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' serif for headlines to reflect a modern adaptation of traditional aesthetics, best suited for titles and shorter text passages. Body font: 'PT Sans', to bring in a bit of modern warmth and personality for readability.
- Use classic, minimalist icons related to literature and nature (e.g., quills, books, leaves).
- Emphasize whitespace and balance, drawing inspiration from classic book design for a polished aesthetic.
- Subtle page transitions to evoke the sensation of turning pages in a cherished book.
